# Customer Service Capacity Planning & Team Staffing Simulator

A practical, interactive tool designed for Customer Service Managers, Team Leads, and Workforce Schedulers to test staff schedules against real-world customer demand. 

Traditional staffing calculators treat phone calls and chat volumes as flat, steady streams. In reality, customers rush in all at once, teams take lunch breaks, and new hires take longer to solve tough tickets. This tool simulates a live 8-hour shift over 12 different "days" to see exactly where your queue will back up, where your team will burn out, and how to fix it.

## 📊 What You Can Model

*   **Your Real Volumes:** Plug in your own support channels, daily volumes, talk times, and wrap-up/notes windows.
*   **Ticket Difficulties:** Divide your traffic into Tier 1 (Simple), Tier 2 (Escalations), and Tier 3 (Advanced/Technical) tickets to see how complex workloads impact your wait times.
*   **The Experience Gap:** Mix and match your roster using Supervisors, Senior Agents, and Junior Interns. The tool automatically accounts for the extra time a newer agent needs to solve tough tickets and write up notes.
*   **Real-World Friction:** Account for lunch breaks, daily huddles, and the inevitable "morning sick call" to find the single points of failure in your schedule.

## 🎯 Instant Operational Insights

Once you click **[Run Stress Test]**, the simulator exposes three easy-to-read dashboards comparing your current roster against a speculative/experimental draft:
1.  **SLA & Speed-to-Serve:** Shows your target SLA achievement, global average wait times, and customer hang-up/abandonment rates.
2.  **Team Utilization & Burnout Index:** Warns you if your schedule forces agents past the safe 85% occupancy redline.
3.  **Skill Alignment Audit:** Pinpoints exactly which ticket tier is causing your holding lines to collapse.
4.  **Plain-English Verdicts:** Translates raw numbers into clear management warnings like `⚠️ INTERN FRAGILITY TRAP` or `🔥 CRITICAL BURNOUT RISK`.
